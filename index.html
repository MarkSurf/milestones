    Innovation Milestones Questionnaire


    One way of measuring the success of innovation is by achieved milestones. For example; in the Netherlands the milestone of 80% of households having an internet connection was reached in 2006. With this questionnaire we make an effort to map how people at Surf think about a wide variety of innovation subjects by asking them when they expect a milestone to be reached, how certain they are about this, and what they think the potential impact for Surf will be.

    Instructions

    The questionnaire consists of a number of technological milestones divided in 8 categories. Each milestone should be rated on three dimensions:

    1. When will this milestone be reached
    2. How certain are you of this date
    3. What will the impact for Surf be

    The milestones were chosen to be as concrete as possible while avoiding to name specific technologies, vendors, suppliers or manufacturers.

    When answering this questionnaire, try not to overthink and use your gut feeling when an answer doesn't immediately come to mind. For the "when" question be sure to enter the answer when you think the milestone is reached first. The impact for Surf can be any kind of impact, like the need to offer services using this innovation or having to change how work is being done. Feel free to answer or skip any amount of questions.

    Answers are saved automatically in a Google Sheet. The questionnaire uses an unique id for each user that is saved locally in a cookie. This id is used to allow you to continue the questionnaire where you left of after returning to the page.
